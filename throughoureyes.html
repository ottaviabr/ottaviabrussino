Through Our Eyes

This project captures the real stories and moments of young migrants living on the island of Samos, Greece. Through their photographs and words, we glimpse a life shaped by hope, hardship, and resilience.

Each image shares a unique perspective, told by the migrants themselves. Their voices guide us beyond statistics and headlines, revealing the human side of this ongoing crisis.

“I remember that day. There was a protest by the African community against the way the camp was being managed. They were asking to be transferred. Some of the toilets were burned” — Mahdi, 15
“This is the boat that carried me and my family in the sea between Turkey and Greece at 3 o’clock in the morning” Samaneh, 16
